# Interview Discussion

## Question

Are design patterns still relevant?

## Weak Answer

Yes, we use all 23 GoF patterns.

## Strong Answer

Yes, as responses to pressure — but fewer patterns appear in daily code. I tier them by how often the pressure appears in our domain and prefer tables, functions, and framework hooks when they are simpler.

## Questions To Ask

- Which patterns appear in your last three PRs?
- Which patterns did you remove last year?

## Key Insight

Modern senior signal: **pattern literacy + restraint**.
