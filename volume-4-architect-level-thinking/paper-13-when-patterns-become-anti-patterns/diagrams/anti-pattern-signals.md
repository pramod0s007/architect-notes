# Anti-Pattern Signals

```text
Pattern applied
        ↓
No growing pressure?
        ↓
Ceremony > clarity
        ↓
Anti-pattern
```

## Quick checks

| Signal | Question |
|--------|----------|
| Strategy Explosion | More interfaces than call paths? |
| Factory Hell | More factories than product types? |
| Visitor | Would a table be shorter? |
| Builder | Fewer than 5 optional fields? |
