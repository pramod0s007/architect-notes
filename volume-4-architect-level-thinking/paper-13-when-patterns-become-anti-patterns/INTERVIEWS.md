# Interview Discussion

## Question

Tell me about a time you removed a pattern.

## Weak Answer

We switched to microservices.

## Strong Answer

Describe measurable pain (onboarding time, test cost), the simpler replacement, and what pressure you would need to see before reintroducing the pattern.

## Questions To Ask

- How many implementations exist today?
- How often does the matrix grow?
- Can a junior read this in one sitting?

## Key Insight

Senior engineers optimize for **removal cost** and **team comprehension**, not pattern coverage.
