# Volume 4 — Creational Patterns and Architect-Level Thinking

This volume covers two things: how objects get created (Builder, Factory), and how architects reason about patterns at a meta level — when to apply them, when NOT to apply them, and how to select the right one.

## The Most Important Paper

**Paper 13** is the most important in the entire series. Knowing when NOT to use a pattern is the primary signal that separates staff engineers from senior engineers.

Read Papers 11–12 for the patterns. Read Papers 13–15 for the judgment.
