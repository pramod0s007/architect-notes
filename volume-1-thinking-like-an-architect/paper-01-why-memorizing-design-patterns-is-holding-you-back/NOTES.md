# Author Notes

## Core Message

Patterns are consequences.

Pressures are causes.

## Key Teaching Points

- Start from the problem, not the pattern.
- Identify the pressure.
- Refactor only when pressure becomes visible.
- Introduce the minimum abstraction necessary.
- Avoid speculative generality.
