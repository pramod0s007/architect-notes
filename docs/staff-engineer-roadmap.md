**Papers:** [03](../volume-1-thinking-like-an-architect/paper-03-the-death-of-if-else/), [04](../volume-2-behavioral-design/paper-04-strategy-pattern-through-real-refactoring/) – [06](../volume-2-behavioral-design/paper-06-command-pattern-through-banking-systems/)

**Practice:** Implement and run samples under `code-samples/`.

## Design

Isolate variation (behavior, state, rules, construction, creation).

**Papers:** [07](../volume-2-behavioral-design/paper-07-visitor-pattern-without-uml/) – [12](../volume-4-architect-level-thinking/paper-12-factory-pattern/)

**Practice:** Classify exercises in [Paper 14](../volume-4-architect-level-thinking/paper-14-pattern-selection-decision-tree/).

## Architecture

Choose abstractions that match **growth rate** and **bounded context**.

**Papers:** [02](../volume-1-thinking-like-an-architect/paper-02-the-four-architectural-buckets/), [09](../volume-3-enterprise-patterns/paper-09-specification-pattern/) – [10](../volume-3-enterprise-patterns/paper-10-chain-of-responsibility/)

**Docs:** [Architecture map](architecture-map.md), [Pattern selection tree](pattern-selection-decision-tree.md)

## Influence

Reviews, RFCs, and mentorship — teach pressure-first thinking.

**Papers:** [13](../volume-4-architect-level-thinking/paper-13-when-patterns-become-anti-patterns/) (maturity curve, warning signs)

**Outcome:** Teams ask "should we use this pattern?" not "which pattern fits?"

## Strategy

Align technical choices with product and organizational strategy.

**Papers:** [15](../volume-4-architect-level-thinking/paper-15-which-patterns-still-matter-in-2026/) (2026 relevance, AI era)

**Outcome:** Pattern literacy supports delivery speed — it does not replace product judgment.
